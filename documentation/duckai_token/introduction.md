# DUCKAI Token

## Introduction

The $DUCKAI token was launched on Pump.fun on October 24th 2024 as a memecoin revolving around the DUCKAI Agent [@duckunfiltered](https://x.com/duckunfiltered) on X. Since then the DUCKAI Labs team has integrated the $DUCKAI Token into rewards program for interacting with the agent and premium access to DUCKAI Labs Chat.

## About DUCKAI Labs

DUCKAI Labs is a team of builders and researchers building the decentralized AI stack for the future of commerce, payments, and agent to agent communications. Our suite includes the the DUCKAI Agent on [X](https://x.com/duckunfiltered), the [DUCKAI Labs Chat](https://app.duckai.ai), and the [OpenPond Network](https://openpond.ai).

## How do i participate in the Ecosystem?

Right now, you can verify your holdings on [DuckAI Labs Chat](https://app.duckai.ai), to get premium access to the top models from OpenAI, xAI, Anthropic and more. Interact with the agent on X and in telegram as part of our [Season 1](#season-1) rewards program, this helps to train our AI models to be more accurate and useful.

- Read more about [Season 1](/documentation/duckai_token/season1) and earn rewards!
- Check out [DUCKAI Labs Chat Web App](https://app.duckai.ai) and get access to the top models from OpenAI, xAI, Anthropic and more. Learn how to [verify your $DUCKAI holdings](/documentation/duckai_token/duckai_chat) to get premium access to the top models from OpenAI, xAI, Anthropic and more.
